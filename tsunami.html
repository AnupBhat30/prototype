Tsunami

What is a Tsunami?

Tsunami is a Japanese word meaning harbour (tsu) wave (nami)
A tsunami is a powerful series of ocean waves generated by significant disturbances, such as underwater earthquakes or volcanic eruptions.

How are Tsunamis caused?

Tsunamis are often triggered by underwater earthquakes, but they can also be caused by volcanic eruptions, landslides, or even meteorite impacts.
